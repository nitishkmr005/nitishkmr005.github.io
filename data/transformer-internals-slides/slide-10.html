Transformer Internals

Architecture

Three transformer families

Encoder-Only

Bidirectional attention for classification and embeddings.

Examples: BERT, RoBERTa.

Decoder-Only

Causal attention for generation and chat.

Examples: GPT-4, LLaMA, Mistral.

Encoder-Decoder

Separate encoder + decoder for seq2seq tasks.

Examples: T5, BART.

Decoder-only dominates because next-token prediction scales cleanly with compute.
