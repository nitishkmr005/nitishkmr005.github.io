Block Recipe

RMSNorm -> RoPE + GQA -> SwiGLU

  • Pre-norm residuals for stability
  • GQA for KV cache savings
  • SwiGLU boosts FFN capacity

Choose by Task

  • Long context: RoPE + scaling or ALiBi
  • Memory bound: GQA or MQA
  • Classification: encoder-only BERT variants
  • Generation: decoder-only GPT style
