### Setup Instructions for Contact Form

To activate the contact form:

1. Sign up for a free account at [Formspree](https://formspree.io/)
2. Create a new form and get your form ID
3. Replace `YOUR_FORM_ID` in the form action URL with your actual form ID
4. (Optional) Customize email notifications and spam protection in Formspree dashboard

**Alternative Options**:
- [Netlify Forms](https://www.netlify.com/products/forms/) (if deploying to Netlify)
- [Google Forms](https://forms.google.com/)
- [Basin](https://usebasin.com/)
